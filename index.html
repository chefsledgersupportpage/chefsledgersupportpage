Common Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Chef's Ledger uses iCloud (CloudKit) to sync your data automatically. Make sure you're signed into iCloud on all your devices. Your data will sync automatically.

How do I share inventory with family or friends?

Use the Import/Export feature in the app. Export your inventory data from the Profile tab, then share the exported file with others. They can import it from their profile tab into their app to access the shared inventory.

Why does the app need camera access?

Camera access is used for the Receipt Scanner feature, which allows you to scan receipts and save them as PDF files. You can disable this in iOS Settings if you don't use this feature.

Why does the app need location access?

Location access is used to find nearby grocery stores when using the Store Finder feature. Location data is not stored permanently. You can disable this in iOS Settings if you don't use this feature.

How do I export my data?

Go to the Profile tab in the app and tap "Export Data" to save all your inventory, shopping lists, and expenses as a JSON file.
